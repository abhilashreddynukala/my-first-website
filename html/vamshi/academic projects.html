BURGULAR ALARAM:
 Burglar alarms have become standard equipment in stores and other businesses, and
they're becoming increasingly common in private homes as well.
If you've ever shopped for a home security system, then you know there are a wide variety of options available. These systems range from do-it-yourself kits you can pick up for $10 to sophisticated whole-house security networks that must be installed by professionals.
But, as it turns out, most alarm systems are actually built around the same basic design concepts.
SURVILLIANCE ROBOT:
 The main purpose of the robot we are making is to provide visual information of hard to access places, for example a building under a hostage situation.
Hence the main feature of our robot is an onboard video camera.
Also the robot must be compact and self-contained in the sense it must have an onboard battery pack and wireless interface to the human controller.
